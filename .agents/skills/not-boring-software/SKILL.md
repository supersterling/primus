# Not Boring Software

A design and product philosophy distilled from Andy Allen's work at [Not Boring Software](https://www.notboring.software) — makers of Not Boring Calculator, Weather, and Camera. Fully bootstrapped, tiny team, no VC pressure. Their apps feel like nothing else on your phone.

The core idea: **design as differentiation**. Not design that explains new technology or helps you scale — design that makes software worth *wanting*, the same way great automotive or fashion design does.

## When to Use This Skill

Use when:
- Designing interactions, onboarding, or UI that should feel tactile and alive
- Making decisions about product scope, speed, or what to cut
- Questioning whether to add AI, new features, or follow a trend
- Trying to give a product a strong point of view or visible authorship
- Prototyping a new idea and not knowing where to start
- Building indie software or a bootstrapped product
- Deciding when to ship
